## Prose first

Body text uses a comfortable system sans, sized to read at the back of a room without feeling shouty up close. Paragraphs flow naturally; line-height leaves enough air to scan.

!muted Muted paragraphs drop a tier in size and saturation — useful for asides, captions, and footnotes.

### A quote, with attribution

:spacer:

> The best presentations feel like a conversation that happens to be illustrated.
> Whatever the framework, the words still have to do most of the work.
> — anonymous, on talks

The renderer detects a trailing `— attribution` line and styles it as a `<cite>` underneath.

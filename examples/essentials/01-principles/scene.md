# What makes a good code review

A useful review is less about catching bugs and more about teaching the shape of the codebase — to the author, and to the next person who reads the diff six months from now.

- Read the whole change before commenting on any line
- Ask questions instead of prescribing fixes
- Praise clarity as loudly as you flag confusion
- Leave the codebase a little more consistent than you found it
